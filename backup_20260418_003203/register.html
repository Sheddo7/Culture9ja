Terms & Agreement
Please read the full terms and conditions carefully. You must accept all agreements before proceeding to payment.

1. Eligibility

Participants must be 18 years or older, Nigerian citizens or individuals of Nigerian descent, and must possess a valid means of identification. Participants must be physically and mentally fit to take part in the show.

2. Registration & Payment

All applicants must complete the official online registration form and provide accurate, truthful information. Registration is only complete after payment of the non-refundable ₦10,000 registration fee through official payment channels. Submitting an application does not guarantee selection.

3. Code of Conduct

All participants must show respect for all cultures, traditions, religions, and ethnic groups. Participants must refrain from hate speech, discrimination, bullying, harassment, or violence. Avoid the use of drugs, excessive alcohol, or illegal substances during participation. Maintain decent dressing in alignment with Nigerian cultural values and comply with all instructions from show producers.

4. Media & Recording Consent

The organizers may record video, audio, and photographs during auditions and production. Such recordings may be used for broadcast, promotion, advertising, and digital media. Contestants will not claim compensation for the use of these recordings unless otherwise agreed in writing.

5. Intellectual Property

All recordings, footage, and media produced during the show remain the property of Culture9ja Reality TV. Contestants may not reproduce or distribute official show content without written permission from Culture9ja Limited.

6. Disqualification

The organizers reserve the right to disqualify any contestant who violates the terms and rules of the show, engages in misconduct or illegal activity, or provides false information during registration. The decision of the show's Executive Committee shall be final.

7. Liability

The organizers of Culture9ja Reality TV shall not be held responsible for any loss, injury, or damage that may occur during auditions or participation. Participants agree to take part at their own risk.

8. Privacy

All personal information provided during registration will be used solely for managing the show and handled in accordance with applicable Nigerian privacy laws. Information shall not be shared with third parties without consent, except as required by law.

9. Anti-Corruption Policy

Bribery, vote manipulation, or favouritism is strictly prohibited. All judges and production staff must maintain neutrality and integrity throughout the competition. Any conflict of interest must be declared immediately.

10. Changes to Terms

Culture9ja Limited reserves the right to modify these terms at any time. Updates will be posted on the official website at www.culture9ja.com.
