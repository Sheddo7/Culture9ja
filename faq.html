Category 1
Eligibility Requirements
1
Who is eligible to apply for Culture9ja Reality TV Show?
▼
To be eligible, you must:
  • Be 18 years or older at the time of registration
  • Be a Nigerian citizen or individual of Nigerian descent
  • Possess a valid means of identification
  • Demonstrate knowledge, talent, or interest in Nigerian culture
  • Be physically and mentally fit to participate in the show
The organisers reserve the right to request proof of age, identity, and eligibility at any time.
2
Can Nigerians living abroad (diaspora) apply?
▼
Yes. Culture9ja specifically welcomes Nigerians living abroad as part of our diaspora engagement strategy. The show offers online audition submission options for participants who cannot attend physical auditions in Nigeria. Diaspora-focused campaigns are also part of our outreach strategy to bring the Nigerian cultural experience to Nigerians worldwide.
3
Is there an upper age limit for applicants?
▼
There is no upper age limit for Culture9ja applicants. The minimum age is 18 years at the time of registration. Culture9ja celebrates cultural heritage across all generations, and participants of all adult age groups are welcome and encouraged to apply.
4
What cultural background or talent do I need to have?
▼
Applicants must demonstrate knowledge, talent, or genuine interest in Nigerian culture. Competition categories include: Traditional Dance, Cultural Fashion, Native Language Oratory, Traditional Music, Nigerian Cuisine, Storytelling & Folklore, and Cultural Crafts. You do not need to be a professional — passion, knowledge, and authentic cultural connection are highly valued.
5
Can someone with a criminal record apply?
▼
Individuals with criminal records involving violence, fraud, or misconduct may be disqualified from participation. All applicants are subject to a background check as part of the screening process. Culture9ja is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and authentic competition environment.
Category 2
Audition Questions
1
How do auditions work?
▼
Auditions for Culture9ja Season 1 are held in multiple cities across Nigeria as well as online. Applicants showcase their cultural knowledge, talent, or skill before a panel of regional judges. Shortlisted candidates advance to the screening stage which includes interviews, cultural assessments, and background checks. Those who pass proceed to bootcamp.
2
Where will auditions be held?
▼
Physical auditions will be conducted across all six geopolitical zones of Nigeria. The nationwide audition tour will cover multiple cities per zone to ensure every state has access. An online audition submission option is also available for those who cannot attend in person. Official audition cities and dates will be announced on www.culture9ja.com and all official social media channels.
3
Do I need to pay before attending an audition?
▼
Yes. A non-refundable registration fee of ₦10,000 is required to complete your application before attending an audition. This fee covers application processing, contestant screening, audition logistics, and administrative costs. Payment must be made through the official payment channels on the Culture9ja website. Please note: Registration fees are non-refundable.
4
What happens if I am shortlisted after auditions?
▼
Shortlisted applicants will be contacted via email and SMS for further stages. The selection process may include: Application review and background check, Screening assessment, In-person auditions, and Interviews. Only shortlisted applicants will be contacted — submitting an application does not guarantee selection.
5
Can I submit an online audition video?
▼
Yes. Culture9ja accepts online audition submissions for applicants who cannot attend physical audition venues — particularly those in the Nigerian diaspora or in states without a nearby audition centre. Your introduction video (uploaded during registration, maximum 3 minutes) forms the basis of your online audition. Full online audition instructions are provided upon registration.
6
When does Season 1 audition tour begin?
▼
The nationwide audition tour dates for Season 1 will be officially announced at the Culture9ja Grand Launch in Abuja, Federal Capital Territory. Audition dates, venues, and schedules will be published on www.culture9ja.com and all official social media platforms. Follow us to stay updated on all announcements.
Category 3
Registration Process
1
How do I register for Culture9ja?
▼
To register, visit the registration page and complete all 6 steps:
  • Personal Information (name, DOB, gender, phone, email, state)
  • Background & Talent (occupation, ethnicity, talent category, bio)
  • Media Uploads (passport photo, introduction video, valid ID)
  • Next of Kin / Guardian details
  • Accept the Terms & Conditions
  • Complete the ₦10,000 registration payment
Your Application Number will be sent to your email and phone after successful payment.
2
What is the registration fee and is it refundable?
▼
The registration fee is ₦10,000 (Ten Thousand Naira only). This fee is non-refundable under all circumstances. It covers application processing, contestant screening, audition logistics, and administrative costs. Payment must be made through the official payment channels on the Culture9ja website — Paystack or Flutterwave.
3
What documents do I need to register?
▼
You will need to upload the following during registration:
  • Passport Photograph — clear, recent photo with plain background (JPG/PNG, max 5MB)
  • Introduction Video — maximum 3 minutes showcasing your cultural talent (MP4/MOV, max 200MB)
  • Valid ID — NIN slip, Voter's card, Driver's license, International passport, or Student ID
You will also need your Next of Kin's full name, address, phone number, and relationship to you.
4
What payment methods are accepted?
▼
The following payment options are accepted:
  • Debit / ATM Card — pay securely online using your Nigerian debit card
  • Bank Transfer — transfer to Culture9ja Limited's official account
  • Online Payment Gateway — via Paystack or Flutterwave
After successful payment, a confirmation message and unique Application Number will be sent to your registered email and phone. Keep your Application Number safe for future reference.
5
I paid but did not receive my confirmation. What should I do?
▼
If you completed payment but did not receive a confirmation email or SMS, please:
  • Check your spam or junk email folder
  • Ensure your email and phone number were entered correctly during registration
  • Wait up to 30 minutes for payment verification to complete
  • If the issue persists, contact us at support@culture9ja.com or call +234 9039997729 with your payment details and reference number
6
Can I update my application after submitting?
▼
Once submitted and paid, applications cannot be edited through the portal. If you need to correct critical information (such as your contact details or uploaded documents), please contact our team immediately at support@culture9ja.com or call 09055567245. Providing false or misleading information in your application may result in disqualification.
Category 4
Voting Procedures
1
How does audience voting work on Culture9ja?
▼
Culture9ja features an active audience voting system that empowers viewers to support their favourite contestants during the live show phase. Voting instructions, eligible contestants, and voting periods will be published on the official Culture9ja website and social media platforms during each live show episode. Voting processes are transparent, fair, and verifiable per our Anti-Corruption Policy.
2
Who can vote for contestants?
▼
Voting is open to members of the public — both within Nigeria and in the Nigerian diaspora globally. The voting system supports digital and social media integration, allowing for nationwide and global participation. Full voting eligibility rules and restrictions per user will be published when the live show phase begins.
3
Is voting free or paid?
▼
Full details on voting costs, platforms, and procedures will be announced when the live show phase commences. Culture9ja is committed to making voting as accessible as possible for Nigerian audiences both at home and in the diaspora. Follow official channels at www.culture9ja.com for all voting announcements.
4
Are there limits on how many times I can vote?
▼
Yes. Voting limits and restrictions per user will be enforced to ensure a fair and transparent competition. Specific voting limits will be published on the official Voting Page when the live show begins. Per our Anti-Corruption and Fairness Policy, vote manipulation or artificial voting is strictly prohibited and may result in disqualification of affected contestants.
5
Can judges be influenced by sponsors or the public to favour specific contestants?
▼
Absolutely not. Culture9ja maintains a strict Anti-Corruption and Fairness Policy. Bribery, vote manipulation, or favouritism is strictly prohibited. Judges and production staff must maintain full neutrality and integrity. Sponsors are explicitly prohibited from influencing judging outcomes. Any conflict of interest must be declared immediately to the Executive Committee. The integrity of the competition is fully protected.
Category 5
Technical Support
1
My file upload failed during registration. What should I do?
▼
If your file upload fails, please check the following:
  • File format: Photos must be JPG or PNG (max 5MB). Videos must be MP4 or MOV (max 200MB). ID documents must be JPG, PNG, or PDF (max 5MB).
  • File size: Reduce file size if it exceeds limits using a compression tool before uploading.
  • Internet connection: Ensure you have a stable connection before uploading large video files.
  • If the problem persists, contact support@culture9ja.com with a description of the error.
2
The registration form is not submitting. What is wrong?
▼
The system validates all required fields before allowing submission. Check the following:
  • All required fields marked with * are filled in correctly
  • Your email address is in a valid format (e.g., name@email.com)
  • Your phone number is valid
  • Uploaded files meet the size and format requirements
  • Your date of birth confirms you are 18 years or older
If the form still does not submit after checking all fields, clear your browser cache and try again, or use a different browser. Contact us if the issue persists.
3
My payment was deducted but my application shows "Pending Payment". What do I do?
▼
This may occur due to a network interruption during payment processing. Please:
  • Wait up to 30 minutes for the payment system to automatically verify and update your status.
  • Check your email for a confirmation message from Paystack/Flutterwave.
  • If your status has not updated after 1 hour, contact support@culture9ja.com with your full name, phone number, email, and payment transaction reference number.
Our team will manually verify and update your application status within 24 hours of your enquiry.
4
I did not receive my Application Number. How do I get it?
▼
Your unique Application Number is sent to your registered email and phone number via SMS after successful payment. If you did not receive it:
  • Check your spam/junk folder for the confirmation email
  • Ensure the email and phone number you registered with are correct
  • Contact support@culture9ja.com or call +234 9039997729 with your full name and payment proof
Keep your Application Number safe — you will need it for all future correspondence regarding your application.

Category 6
General Questions
1
What is Culture9ja Reality TV Show?
▼
Culture9ja Reality TV Show is a cultural-based reality television program designed to showcase, preserve, and celebrate the rich heritage, traditions, creativity, and diversity of Nigeria. It is a structured cultural competition broadcast on television and digital platforms, featuring contestants from different Nigerian states and ethnic groups competing through culturally themed challenges and performances. Culture9ja Limited — the company behind the show — is registered in the Federal Republic of Nigeria and operates under National Broadcasting Commission (NBC) guidelines.
2
Where will the show be broadcast?
▼
Culture9ja will be broadcast across multiple platforms:
  • National and regional television stations across Nigeria
  • YouTube — official Culture9ja channel
  • Official website — www.culture9ja.com
  • Social media — Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, X (Twitter)
Broadcast partnerships are currently being finalised. All broadcasting announcements will be made at the Grand Launch in Abuja, FCT.
3
When does Season 1 officially launch?
▼
The Official Launch and Grand Opening of Culture9ja Reality TV Show will take place in Abuja, Federal Capital Territory — the capital city of Nigeria. The exact date will be officially announced on the Culture9ja website and social media platforms. Previous major events include the unveiling ceremonies in Benue State and Jos, Plateau State, as well as the Culture9ja 2025 Cultural Economic Summit in Jos.
4
How can brands and organisations partner with Culture9ja?
▼
Culture9ja Limited welcomes sponsorship and partnership enquiries from corporations, government bodies, media organisations, and cultural institutions. We offer tiered sponsorship packages including Title Sponsor, Gold Sponsor, and Silver/Brand Partner levels — each with specific brand visibility benefits across TV broadcast, digital platforms, live events, and the annual Cultural Economic Summit. Contact us at support@culture9ja.com or visit our Sponsors & Partners page for full details.
