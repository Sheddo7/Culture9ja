About the Show

Reality TV That Actually Reflects Who We Are

Nigerians have begun turning back to their roots, hungry for entertainment that reflects their true cultural heritage. From traditional music and dance to folklore and indigenous storytelling, there is a renewed desire to preserve what makes us distinct.

"Culture9ja is a cultural-based reality television program designed to showcase, preserve, and celebrate the rich heritage, traditions, creativity, and diversity of Nigeria."

Launching at a time when audiences are demanding authenticity, Culture9ja entertains while preserving the soul of Nigeria's many communities. This is not just television — it is a movement.
