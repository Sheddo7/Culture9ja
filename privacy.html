Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy explains how Culture9ja Limited collects, stores, uses, and protects personal data provided by participants, website visitors, and other users of Culture9ja services and platforms.
Effective: April 2026 Culture9ja Limited CAMA Registered · Nigeria
Section 1
Introduction

Culture9ja Limited is committed to protecting the privacy of all individuals who interact with our programmes, website, and services. This policy applies to all participants, registered applicants, website visitors, and any person who provides personal information to Culture9ja Limited in connection with the Culture9ja Reality TV Show.

Culture9ja Limited is duly registered in the Federal Republic of Nigeria in accordance with the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA). We operate under applicable Nigerian media, entertainment, broadcasting, and data protection regulations.

Section 2
Data We Collect

During the registration and participation process, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Personal details: Full name, date of birth, gender, state of origin, current location
  • Contact information: Phone number, email address
  • Professional information: Occupation, social media handles, short biography
  • Talent information: Cultural background, talent category, what makes you unique
  • Media uploads: Passport photograph, introduction video, valid identification document
  • Next of Kin information: Full name, address, phone number, occupation, relationship to applicant
  • Payment information: Payment transaction references (card details are processed by Paystack/Flutterwave and not stored by Culture9ja)
Section 3
How We Use Your Data

Personal information provided during registration will be used solely for managing the show and related Culture9ja activities, including:

  • Processing and reviewing your application for the show
  • Contacting shortlisted applicants regarding auditions and further stages
  • Verifying payment and issuing Application Numbers
  • Managing contestant welfare, health, and safety during production
  • Publishing contestant profiles on the Culture9ja website (with your consent)
  • Sending updates, announcements, and instructions via email and SMS
Section 4
Data Storage & Security

Personal data of participants shall be collected and stored securely in accordance with applicable Nigerian privacy laws. Culture9ja Limited implements appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Access to participant data within our team is restricted to staff members who require it for their specific role in managing the show's operations.

Section 5
Third Parties

Information shall not be shared with third parties without consent, except as required by law. We may share your data with:

  • Payment processors (Paystack / Flutterwave) for payment verification purposes only
  • Broadcast partners where required for production purposes, under confidentiality obligations
  • Law enforcement or regulatory bodies if required by applicable Nigerian law

Sponsors shall not receive personal data of participants. Sponsor involvement is limited to brand visibility arrangements that do not include access to participant information.

Section 6
Broadcast Consent

"Participants acknowledge that recorded content — including their image, voice, name, and cultural performances — will be publicly broadcast on television and digital platforms as part of the Culture9ja Reality TV Show."

By completing registration and accepting these terms, participants grant Culture9ja Limited the right to use their likeness, name, voice, and performances for broadcast, promotional, and advertising purposes. This consent covers national television broadcast, the official website, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and X (Twitter).

Section 7
Your Rights

As a participant or applicant, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Contact us regarding any data concerns at support@culture9ja.com

Please note that once production and broadcast have commenced, certain data (including recorded footage) cannot be withdrawn from broadcast as it forms part of the show's content, to which you have provided consent.

Section 8
Amendments

Culture9ja Limited reserves the right to amend this Privacy Policy when necessary. All participants will be notified of significant changes. Updates will be posted on the official website at www.culture9ja.com. Continued participation after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
