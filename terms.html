Culture9ja Reality TV Show — Contestant Terms & Conditions. By applying to participate in this programme, you agree to comply with the following Terms and Conditions in full.

Section 1
Eligibility

To be eligible to participate in Culture9ja Reality TV Show, you must:

  • Be 18 years or older at the time of registration
  • Be a citizen or legal resident of Nigeria
  • Possess a valid means of identification
  • Be physically and mentally fit to participate in the show

The organisers reserve the right to request proof of age, identity, and eligibility at any time. Individuals with criminal records involving violence, fraud, or misconduct may be disqualified from participation.

Section 2
Registration
  • All applicants must complete the official online registration form
  • Applicants must provide accurate and truthful information
  • Any false or misleading information may result in disqualification
  • Registration will only be considered complete after payment of the registration fee
Section 3
Registration Fee

Please Note: A non-refundable registration fee of ₦10,000 (Ten Thousand Naira) is required to process your application. Registration fees are non-refundable under all circumstances.

  • Payment must be made through the official payment channels on the Culture9ja website
  • Payment confirmation will be required before the application is reviewed
  • Accepted payment methods: Debit / ATM Card, Bank Transfer, Paystack / Flutterwave gateway
Section 4
Selection Process

Submitting an application does not guarantee selection. The selection process may include:

  • Application review and background check
  • Screening assessment
  • Auditions
  • Interviews

Only shortlisted applicants will be contacted for further stages via email and SMS.

Section 5
Code of Conduct

All contestants are expected to:

  • Show respect to fellow contestants, organisers, and production staff
  • Show respect for all cultures, traditions, religions, and ethnic groups
  • Avoid violence, harassment, or abusive behaviour
  • Refrain from hate speech, discrimination, or bullying
  • Avoid the use of drugs, excessive alcohol, or illegal substances during participation
  • Maintain decent dressing in alignment with Nigerian cultural values
  • Follow all rules and instructions given by the production team

Violation of the Code of Conduct may result in warnings, penalties, suspension, or immediate disqualification.

Section 6
Media & Recording Consent
  • The organisers may record video, audio, and photographs during auditions and production
  • Such recordings may be used for broadcast, promotion, advertising, and digital media
  • Contestants will not claim compensation for the use of these recordings unless otherwise agreed in writing
  • Participants grant Culture9ja the right to use their likeness, name, voice, and performances for promotional purposes
Section 7
Intellectual Property
  • All recordings, footage, and media produced during the show remain the property of Culture9ja Reality TV and its producers
  • Contestants may not reproduce or distribute official show content without written permission from Culture9ja Limited
  • Cultural performances must respect traditional ownership and community intellectual rights where applicable
  • All intellectual property, trademarks, and branded assets of Culture9ja Limited are officially registered and protected under Nigerian intellectual property law
Section 8
Disqualification

The organisers reserve the right to disqualify any contestant who:

  • Violates the terms and rules of the show
  • Engages in misconduct or illegal activity
  • Provides false information during registration

Minor infractions may result in a formal warning. Serious misconduct may lead to immediate disqualification. The decision of the show's Executive Committee shall be final. Appeals may be submitted in writing within 48 hours of disciplinary action.

Section 9
Liability

The organisers of Culture9ja Reality TV shall not be held responsible for any loss, injury, or damage that may occur during auditions or participation. Participants agree to take part at their own risk.

The show shall provide safe accommodation and a secure environment for participants. Medical support shall be available during production. Participants must report health or safety concerns immediately to the production team.

Section 10
Privacy

All personal information provided during registration will be used solely for managing the show and handled in accordance with applicable Nigerian privacy laws. Personal data of participants shall be collected and stored securely. Information shall not be shared with third parties without consent, except as required by law.
