Official Launch Announced — Culture9ja Reality TV Show Comes to Abuja, Federal Capital Territory
The grand opening and official launch of Culture9ja Reality TV Show will take place in Nigeria's capital city, Abuja, Federal Capital Territory. The event marks the culmination of months of preparation, nationwide unveiling ceremonies, and community engagement — and signals the beginning of Season 1 of Nigeria's first cultural reality television show. Applications for Season 1 contestants are open across all 36 states. The show promises to celebrate, preserve, and showcase the extraordinary cultural diversity of Nigeria — 250 ethnic groups, one unforgettable stage.
