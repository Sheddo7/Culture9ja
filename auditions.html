Audition Overview

How to Participate

Culture9ja Reality TV Show is open to all Nigerian citizens and individuals of Nigerian descent who are 18 years and above. Whether you are a dancer, musician, storyteller, fashion enthusiast, culinary artist, or bearer of indigenous knowledge — if you have a cultural talent or deep passion for Nigerian heritage, there is a place for you on this stage.

Participation begins with completing the official online registration form and paying the non-refundable registration fee of ₦10,000. Shortlisted applicants will then be invited to attend physical auditions in their nearest city or submit an online audition video.

"Culture9ja is designed to showcase, preserve, and celebrate the rich heritage, traditions, creativity, and diversity of Nigeria — one talent at a time."

Register Online

Eligibility Requirements

Who Can Apply

01
Nigerian Citizenship
Participants must be Nigerian citizens or individuals of Nigerian descent. Proof of identity and nationality must be provided during the application process.
02
Age Requirement
All applicants must be 18 years of age or older at the time of registration. A valid means of identification confirming your date of birth is mandatory.
03
Cultural Knowledge or Talent
Participants must demonstrate knowledge, talent, or genuine interest in Nigerian culture — including traditional dance, music, language, cuisine, fashion, crafts, or storytelling.
04
Official Registration
Contestants must complete the official application form and provide valid identification. Your application is only confirmed after payment of the registration fee.
05
Physical & Mental Fitness
Participants must be physically and mentally fit to take part in the show. The organizers reserve the right to request proof of fitness and eligibility at any stage.
06
Accurate Information
All information provided during registration must be accurate and truthful. Any false or misleading information may result in immediate disqualification.
Disqualification Notice
Individuals with criminal records involving violence, fraud, or serious misconduct may be disqualified from participation. The organizers reserve the right to conduct background checks and to disqualify any applicant whose participation is deemed contrary to the values and objectives of Culture9ja Reality TV Show.

Online Audition Option

Audition from Anywhere

Can't make it to a physical centre? Culture9ja provides a fully supported online audition pathway — ensuring no eligible Nigerian, whether at home or in the diaspora, is excluded from participating.

1
Complete Online Registration
Visit culture9ja.com and fill in the official registration form with your personal details, cultural background, talent description, and next of kin information.
2
Pay the Registration Fee
Pay the non-refundable ₦10,000 registration fee via debit card or secure online payment gateway (Paystack / Flutterwave). You will receive an Application Number upon confirmation.
3
Record Your Audition Video
Record a clear, well-lit video (minimum 2 minutes, maximum 5 minutes) showcasing your cultural talent. Introduce yourself, state your tribe and state of origin, then perform or present your talent.
4
Upload Supporting Materials
Upload your passport photograph, introduction video, audition performance video, and valid identification through the submission portal. Ensure all files meet the required format and size.
5
Await Review & Feedback
Your submission will be reviewed by the Culture9ja judging panel. Shortlisted applicants will be contacted via email and SMS with instructions for the next stage.
