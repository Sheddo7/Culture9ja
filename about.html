Show Overview

Reality TV That Actually Reflects Who We Are

Nigeria is home to one of the most diverse and vibrant cultural landscapes in the world, boasting over 500 distinct languages and ethnic traditions. Yet, in the age of rapid globalisation and digital entertainment, many of these traditions risk being diluted or forgotten.

Culture9ja Reality TV Show was conceived as a direct response to this cultural challenge. It is a purpose-driven entertainment platform designed to celebrate what makes Nigeria uniquely great — its people, heritage, creativity, and diversity.

"Culture9ja is a cultural-based reality television program designed to showcase, preserve, and celebrate the rich heritage, traditions, creativity, and diversity of Nigeria."

The show brings together talented individuals from across Nigeria who possess deep knowledge of, passion for, or exceptional talent in Nigerian cultural arts. Through structured competitions, cultural challenges, storytelling, and performances, contestants showcase the beauty of their ethnic backgrounds while connecting with audiences across Nigeria and the world.

Culture9ja Limited is a Nigerian cultural entertainment company established to champion the celebration, preservation, and promotion of Nigeria's rich and diverse cultural heritage through innovative television programming. This is not just television — it is a movement.

Competition Format

How the Show Works

The Culture9ja Reality TV Show follows a season-based format spanning 12–16 weeks. Contestants from different Nigerian states and ethnic groups compete through a series of culturally themed challenges and performances, with the winner determined by a combination of judges' scores and audience votes.

Participant Guidelines

Rules & Regulations

All participants are required to comply with the following guidelines. Participation in Culture9ja Reality TV Show constitutes acceptance of these rules and all applicable terms and conditions.

1
Eligibility Requirements
▲
  • Must be 18 years or older at the time of registration
  • Must be a Nigerian citizen or individual of Nigerian descent
  • Must possess a valid means of identification
  • Must demonstrate knowledge, talent, or interest in Nigerian culture
  • Must be physically and mentally fit to participate in the show
  • Individuals with criminal records involving violence, fraud, or misconduct may be disqualified
2
Registration & Application
▼
  • All applicants must complete the official online registration form in full
  • Applicants must provide accurate and truthful information — false details lead to disqualification
  • Registration is only complete after payment of the non-refundable ₦10,000 registration fee
  • Payment must be made through official channels on the website only
  • Submitting an application does not guarantee selection for the show
3
Code of Conduct
▼
  • Show respect for all cultures, traditions, religions, and ethnic groups at all times
  • Refrain from hate speech, discrimination, bullying, harassment, or violence of any kind
  • Avoid the use of drugs, excessive alcohol, or illegal substances during participation
  • Maintain decent dressing in alignment with Nigerian cultural values
  • Comply with all instructions from show producers and management
  • Respect fellow contestants, organizers, judges, and all production staff
4
Content & Performance Guidelines
▼
  • All content must align with Nigerian cultural values and national broadcasting regulations
  • The show shall avoid explicit sexual content, graphic violence, or offensive material
  • Cultural presentations must be authentic and not deliberately misrepresent any ethnic group
  • Producers reserve the right to edit content for clarity, quality, and regulatory compliance
  • Cultural performances must respect traditional ownership and community intellectual rights
5
Media & Intellectual Property
▼
  • All recorded footage and materials produced remain the property of Culture9ja Reality TV
  • Contestants grant the show the right to use their likeness, name, voice, and performances for promotional purposes
  • Contestants may not reproduce or distribute official show content without written permission
  • Recordings may be used for broadcast, promotion, advertising, and digital media without additional compensation
6
Disciplinary Procedures
▼
  • Minor infractions may result in a formal warning issued by the production team
  • Serious misconduct may lead to immediate disqualification from the competition
  • The decision of the show's Executive Committee is final on all disciplinary matters
  • Appeals may be submitted in writing within 48 hours of disciplinary action
  • Disqualification grounds include: rules violation, misconduct, illegal activity, or false registration information
7
Safety, Welfare & Privacy
▼
  • The show shall provide safe accommodation and a secure environment for all participants
  • Medical and psychological support shall be available during production
  • Participants must report health or safety concerns immediately to the production team
  • Personal data of participants shall be collected and stored securely per Nigerian privacy laws
  • Information shall not be shared with third parties without consent, except as required by law
8
Anti-Corruption & Fairness Policy
▼
  • Bribery, vote manipulation, or favouritism is strictly prohibited throughout the competition
  • All judges and production staff must maintain neutrality and integrity at all times
  • Any conflict of interest must be declared immediately to the Executive Committee
  • Sponsors shall not influence judging outcomes under any circumstances
  • Voting processes must be transparent, fair, and verifiable
